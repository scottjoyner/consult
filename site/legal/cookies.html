Cookie Notice

Effective 1 October 2024

How cookies support our workflow

This Cookie Notice describes how Campbell Cognition Consultants LLC (“Campbell Cognition,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies, local storage, pixels, and similar technologies on our website and client portals.

  • Essential cookies: Power booking flows, sign-in sessions, and security safeguards. These load automatically.
  • Analytics cookies: Activate only after you grant consent through our banner and feed aggregated metrics in Neo4j.
  • Your control: Manage preferences from the banner, clear browser storage, or email privacy@campbellcognition.com.

1. What Are Cookies & Similar Technologies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. We also use HTML5 local storage, session storage, and tracking pixels to remember preferences, secure sessions, and measure conversions.

2. Cookies We Use

The table below summarizes the cookies and storage keys we rely on.

Name Purpose Type Retention
ccc_session Maintains secure booking and portal sessions. Essential Session
ccc_consent Records your analytics consent decision. Preference (local storage) 12 months
ccc_analytics_id Assigns a pseudonymous ID for consented analytics events stored in Neo4j. Analytics 6 months
stripe_mid Facilitates secure payment checkout via Stripe. Essential (third-party) 1 year
gcl_au Measures ad conversions when applicable. Only set if you consent to analytics. Analytics (third-party) 90 days

3. How We Use Analytics

When you accept analytics cookies, our site initializes a pseudonymous session identifier and records events such as page visits, CTA clicks, bookings, and engagement with the Campbell Companion workspace. Events are stored in Neo4j to power dashboards and funnel reporting. We review data in aggregate and do not use analytics cookies for behavioral advertising.

4. Managing Your Preferences

  • Interact with the on-site banner to accept or decline analytics cookies at any time.
  • Clear your browser cookies and local storage or use browser privacy modes to remove stored identifiers.
  • Contact us at privacy@campbellcognition.com for assistance or to request deletion of analytics data tied to your consent ID.

5. Updates

We may update this Cookie Notice to reflect changes in technology, our analytics stack, or legal requirements. Updated versions will carry a new effective date and be posted on this page.

6. Contact

Questions about this Cookie Notice or our analytics practices can be sent to privacy@campbellcognition.com.
