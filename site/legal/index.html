Legal Summary & Usage Guide

Updated 1 October 2024

Quick reference

Campbell Cognition Consultants LLC operates on a transparent document stack. Use this page to understand which document governs each step of our consulting workflow and what we expect from each other.

  • Terms & Conditions: Master legal agreement covering scope, payments, IP, confidentiality, and dispute resolution.
  • Privacy Policy: How we collect, use, and secure personal data across engagements.
  • Cookie Notice: Consent-driven analytics and session management details.
  • Retainer Agreement: Monthly advisory cadence, deliverable expectations, and communication rhythm.
  • Proposals / SOWs: Engagement-specific scope, deliverables, milestones, and investment.

Engagement lifecycle

  1. Discovery call booking
    Pay the clarity call fee, accept site terms, and receive onboarding materials.
  2. Proposal delivery
    We summarize goals, risks, deliverables, and pricing in a tartan-branded proposal. Acceptance forms the Engagement Document.
  3. Legal alignment
    Terms, Privacy Policy, Cookie Notice, and (if applicable) the Retainer Agreement govern the relationship.
  4. Delivery & collaboration
    We collaborate via the Campbell Companion workspace, track time and milestones, and issue status updates.
  5. Wrap-up & transition
    Final artifacts, recommendations, and next steps are packaged; post-engagement support is scheduled as needed.

Document applicability

Document When it applies Key expectations
Terms & Conditions All consulting work, from booking through final deliverables. Timely access, prompt payment, confidentiality, and Virginia governing law.
Privacy Policy Whenever we process personal data for marketing, booking, or engagements. Informed consent, data rights, and security commitments.
Cookie Notice When you visit our website or client portals. Consent-based analytics and ability to manage preferences.
Retainer Agreement For ongoing monthly advisory relationships. Monthly allocation, rollover policy, status rhythm, and cancellation terms.
Proposal / SOW For specific initiatives, pilots, or projects. Detailed scope, deliverables, timeline, and acceptance criteria.

Working together

What you can expect from us

  • Senior-level consulting with a bias toward actionable deliverables.
  • Documented architecture decisions, automation blueprints, and compliance-aware guidance.
  • Transparent time tracking, weekly status notes, and clear escalation paths.
  • Stewardship of confidential information aligned with our privacy and security commitments.

What we need from you

  • A single empowered point of contact for prioritization and approvals.
  • Access to relevant systems, documentation, and stakeholders according to the engagement plan.
  • Prompt feedback on deliverables within the review windows stated in the proposal or retainer cadence.
  • Payment of invoices within the agreed terms and notification of any procurement requirements.

Questions & escalation

Need clarifications or custom terms? Email legal@campbellcognition.com or reach your engagement lead directly. We are happy to sign mutual NDAs, provide data processing addenda, or coordinate with your compliance team.
