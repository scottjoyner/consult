Retainer Agreement

Updated 1 October 2024

Usage summary

This retainer agreement (“Agreement”) sets the cadence, services, payment schedule, and expectations for ongoing advisory work between Campbell Cognition Consultants LLC (“Campbell Cognition,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) and the client organization identified in the associated order form or statement of work (“Client,” “you,” or “your”). It incorporates the Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Notice.

  • Cadence: Monthly advisory pods with rolling strategic and implementation support.
  • Allocation: Base package includes 16 consulting hours per month with optional add-on blocks.
  • Communication: Weekly status summaries, shared task board, and direct Slack/Teams channel.
  • Flexibility: 25% rollover buffer, ability to pause for up to 30 days with written notice.

1. Parties & Effective Date

This Agreement is effective on the start date listed in the order form or the first day services commence, whichever occurs first. The Agreement continues on a month-to-month basis until terminated under Section 11.

2. Scope of Services

We provide the ongoing services detailed in the order form, which may include:

  • Architecture and platform advisory for cloud, API, data, and security domains.
  • AI and automation integration strategy, implementation guidance, and model governance.
  • Workflow orchestration, n8n automation design, and operational playbooks.
  • Executive enablement sessions, stakeholder workshops, and documentation reviews.

Any project-specific deliverables beyond the retainer scope will be captured through a supplemental statement of work.

3. Cadence & Collaboration

  • Weekly 45-minute standing sync for status, prioritization, and decision logs.
  • Shared task board (Notion or your selected tool) for backlog, in-flight work, and approvals.
  • Dedicated Slack/Teams channel with same-business-day responses during U.S. Eastern working hours.
  • Monthly executive summary capturing accomplishments, open risks, and next month’s focus.

4. Time Allocation & Rollover

  • Base retainer includes 16 hours per month. Additional hours may be purchased in 8-hour blocks at the rate listed in the order form.
  • Unused time automatically expires at the end of the month; up to 25% of monthly hours may roll over to the subsequent month when requested before month-end.
  • We maintain a shared log of consumed hours accessible through the client portal.

5. Fees & Payment

  • Monthly retainer fees are invoiced in advance and due within 15 calendar days of the invoice date.
  • Overages or pre-approved add-on blocks are invoiced in arrears with the next monthly statement.
  • Payments may be made via ACH, wire transfer, or credit card (3% processing fee applies to card payments).
  • Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month (or the maximum amount allowed by law if lower). We may pause services for accounts more than 15 days past due.

6. Change Requests

We will evaluate any requested changes to scope or cadence within two business days. Material changes may require an updated order form or supplemental statement of work. Work outside the retainer scope begins only after written approval.

7. Client Responsibilities

  • Designate a primary point of contact with authority to prioritize work and accept deliverables.
  • Provide timely access to systems, documentation, and stakeholders necessary to perform services.
  • Inform us of compliance, security, or procurement requirements that impact delivery.
  • Review deliverables within five business days (unless otherwise stated). Feedback delays may affect timelines.

8. Confidentiality & Security

Both parties will protect confidential information according to the Terms & Conditions. We adhere to principle-of-least-privilege access, enforce multi-factor authentication, and use encrypted communication channels. Security incidents will be reported to your point of contact within 48 hours of confirmation.

9. Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, you receive a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use and modify deliverables created specifically for you. We retain ownership of our pre-existing materials, tools, and general know-how, and may reuse non-confidential learnings in other engagements.

10. Compliance & Data Processing

We will align our work with the compliance obligations identified by you (e.g., SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR). If a formal data processing agreement or business associate agreement is required, we will execute the appropriate addendum. We do not act as a system of record for your data.

11. Term, Pause, & Termination

  • This Agreement renews automatically each month. Either party may terminate with 30 days’ written notice after the initial three-month commitment.
  • You may request a pause of up to 30 consecutive days once per calendar year with 10 days’ notice. Paused months do not incur retainer fees; unused hours do not accrue during the pause.
  • Either party may terminate immediately for material breach that remains uncured 14 days after written notice, or for insolvency.

12. Effect of Termination

Upon termination, we will deliver any in-progress artifacts paid for through the effective date and provide a transition summary. Outstanding fees and approved expenses become immediately due. Each party will return or destroy confidential information per the Terms & Conditions.

13. Points of Contact

Operational questions: hello@campbellcognition.com
Legal notices: legal@campbellcognition.com
Security incidents: security@campbellcognition.com

14. Acceptance

By signing the associated order form or paying the retainer invoice, the Client acknowledges reading and agreeing to this Agreement and the incorporated policies. Digital signatures and electronic approvals are deemed originals.
